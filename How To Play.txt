-Description-

The goal of the game is help our hero over to the other side, by tumble a brige of the rigth length.

-Instructions-

Hold SPACE to increase bridge length , Let go of SPACE to tumble the brigde. 

Tryk TAB for show/hide highscore list.

Tryk 'R' to restart the game when you lose.

Tryk 'T' to add your score to the highscore list, when you have lost. ATTENTION:you have write your name in the console window!
